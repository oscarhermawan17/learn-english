## Simple Present

### Formula for sentence
| Subject     | Verb      | Noun / Adj  |
| ----------- |:---------:|:-----------:|
| I           | Verb 1    | Noun / Adj  | 
| You         | Verb 1    | Noun / Adj  | 
| They        | Verb 1    | Noun / Adj  |  
| We          | Verb 1    | Noun / Adj  |
| He          | Verb 1 + s/es   | Noun / Adj  |
| She         | Verb 1 + s/es   | Noun / Adj  |
| It          | Verb 1 + s/es   | Noun / Adj  |
